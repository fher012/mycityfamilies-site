MyCityFamilies Privacy Policy

This page explains, in general terms, how MyCityFamilies handles information you provide when using the app and website.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as your email address, account details, support requests, and feedback you submit through the app or website.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate and improve MyCityFamilies, respond to support requests, maintain account access, review app performance, and communicate important updates related to the service.

Content and Listings

MyCityFamilies may display family-related event and place information gathered from external sources. We may review and update that information to improve accuracy and user experience.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers who help us operate the app or website, or when required by law.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

Your Choices

You can contact us to ask questions about your information, request updates where appropriate, or close your account if account functionality is available through the app.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact support@mycityfamilies.com.
